Crossfire

Medium-sized map • 3 lanes
AD PLACEHOLDER — Banner

Crossfire is a classic 3-lane map. A-long leads to A site, B-short leads to B site, and mid connects everything. Controlling mid lets you rotate faster than the enemy.

Key Positions

  • A-long: Long straight with boxes for cover
  • Mid: Dangerous cross-section, smoke to cross
  • B-short: Tight corridor, easy to hold with shotgun
  • CT spawn: Central rotate point
  • T spawn: Three paths open to you

Smoke Lineups

From T spawn, aim at the third wire on the lamppost and throw a standing smoke to cover mid. From B-short, aim at the edge of the roof and jump-throw for a B-site smoke.

Always smoke mid as attacker. Even a bad smoke is better than no smoke.
