Best Sensitivity Settings (2026)

Updated June 16, 2026 · 8 min read
AD PLACEHOLDER — Leaderboard

Getting your sensitivity right is the fastest way to improve your aim. Most players use settings that are way too high. Here is how to dial yours in.

The Golden Range

90% of pro players use 400-800 DPI with in-game sensitivity between 1.5 and 3.0. This gives you a good balance of flick speed and precision.

  • Start at 800 DPI / 2.0 in-game
  • Adjust so a full mousepad swipe = 180 turn
  • Play 5 deathmatch rounds without changing
  • Fine-tune by 0.1 increments until it feels natural

Pro Player Settings

  • Shroud: 400 DPI / 2.5 sens
  • S1mple: 800 DPI / 1.8 sens
  • TenZ: 800 DPI / 2.0 sens
  • Average pro: 800 DPI / 2.2 sens

ADS & Scope Multiplier

Your ADS sensitivity should be slightly lower than your hipfire. Start at 0.85 and adjust. Scope multiplier should be 0.6-0.8 for snipers.

Lower sensitivity improves precision at the cost of mobility. If you entry-frag, go higher. If you AWP, go lower.
