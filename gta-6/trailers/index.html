The first GTA 6 trailer dropped on December 4, 2023 and became the most-viewed video game trailer in history, surpassing 100 million views in just 3 days. Here is our breakdown of every frame.

Trailer 1 Breakdown

  • Setting: Vice City / Leonida with modern graphics
  • Protagonists: Lucia (first female protagonist) and Jason
  • Bonnie & Clyde theme: robberies, car chases, shootouts
  • Social media: in-game TikTok/Instagram style content
  • Wildlife: alligators, flamingos, dolphins
  • Nightlife: clubs, bars, beach parties
  • Missions: heists, racing, drug trades, jet ski chases
