GTA 6 Leaks — Map, Gameplay & Story Details

The 2022 leak revealed 90+ videos

AD PLACEHOLDER — Leaderboard

In September 2022, one of the biggest video game leaks in history hit the internet. A hacker released 90+ videos of early GTA 6 development footage. Here is what the leaks revealed.

What the Leaks Showed

  • Two protagonists: Lucia and Jason
  • Vice City setting with modern graphics
  • Dynamic NPC interactions
  • Realistic physics and ragdoll
  • Heist and robbery mechanics
  • Car customization
  • Boating and water physics
  • Shooting and combat mechanics

Map Leaks

Leaked footage and data mining suggest the map will be significantly larger than GTA V. The game is expected to feature Vice City and surrounding areas, multiple islands, swamps, beaches, and suburban towns.

Remember: leaked content is from early development. The final game may look completely different.
