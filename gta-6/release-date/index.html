GTA 6 Release Date — 2026 Updates

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Finding GTA 6's release date is the #1 searched gaming question right now. Here is the latest official information and what industry insiders are saying.

Official Release Window

Rockstar Games and Take-Two Interactive have officially stated that GTA 6 is targeting a Fall 2025 release. However, as of mid-2026, many industry analysts believe the game has been pushed internally to early-to-mid 2026. Take-Two CEO has repeatedly reaffirmed confidence in their release timeline in earnings calls.

What We Know

  • First trailer confirmed Fall 2025 window
  • No official delay announced as of June 2026
  • Rockstar has a history of internal delays
  • PS5 and Xbox Series X|S at launch
  • PC version expected 12-18 months later

Pre-Order Status

Pre-orders have not officially opened yet. Rockstar will likely open pre-orders 3-6 months before launch, probably alongside a second trailer or gameplay reveal. When pre-orders go live, expect multiple editions: Standard ($69.99), Deluxe, and Collector Edition.

If you want to be notified the moment pre-orders open, follow Rockstar Games on social media and sign up for their newsletter.
