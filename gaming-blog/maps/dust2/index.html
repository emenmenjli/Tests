Dust2

Large map • Open layout
AD PLACEHOLDER — Banner

Dust2 is an open map that rewards aim duels. Long-range fights are common, so bring a rifle with a scope or a sniper.

Key Positions

  • A-long: The main aim-duel lane
  • Mid: Doors and window peek
  • B-tunnels: Tight close-range fights
  • Catwalk: Connects mid to A site

Tips

On attack, take mid control first before committing to a site. On defense, the A-long awper is critical. Smoke B-tunnels to delay the rush. Position yourself for crossfires and trade kills.
