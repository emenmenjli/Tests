Crossfire Map Guide

Updated June 23, 2026 · 10 min read
AD PLACEHOLDER — Leaderboard

Crossfire is the most played map in Blood Strike. Knowing the angles and timings gives you a massive advantage over opponents who just run in blindly.

Attack Strategy (A Site)

A-long push needs coordination. The defender often holds from the corner or the box head-glitch. Flash early, clear the corner, then plant behind the half-wall for cover.

  • Flash A-long immediately after spawn
  • Clear the corner (common hold position)
  • Plant behind the half-wall
  • Watch flank from CT-side stairs

Defense Strategy (B Site)

B-short is tighter and easier to hold with a sniper or shotgun. Use the box head-glitch and call for rotates as soon as you hear multiple steps.

  • Sniper on B-short boxes
  • Smoke the entrance to delay plant
  • Flash when you hear them pushing
  • Rotate through CT if you have time

Mid Control

Control mid and you control the map. As attacker, you can split to either site. As defender, hold mid passively with an AWP and fall back when outnumbered.

If you win mid control as attacker, you can split to either site. As defender, hold mid passively and fall back when pressured.
