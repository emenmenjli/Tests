Ranked Mode: Climb to Grandmaster

Updated June 14, 2026 · 14 min read
AD PLACEHOLDER — Leaderboard

Ranked is where the real game begins. Climbing requires more than just aim. You need game sense, economy management, and team coordination.

1. Economy Management

The single biggest difference between hard-stuck players and climbers is econ discipline. Do not buy every round. Save with your team and synchronize your buys.

  • Round 1: Pistol + armor or utility
  • Round 2: If you won, SMG + armor. If you lost, save.
  • Round 3: Full buy if you won round 2. Save if you lost.
  • Never buy when your team is saving

2. Team Coordination

Use your mic. Call out enemy positions, damage dealt, and your intent. A team that communicates wins 70% of their matches.

3. Mindset

If you lose two rounds in a row, take a 5-minute break. Tilt is the #1 reason players lose ranked progress. Stay positive, focus on your own performance.

Warm up for 10 minutes in deathmatch before queuing ranked. It raises your win rate by an estimated 15%.
