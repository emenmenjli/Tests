Best Weapon Builds (Season 4)

Updated June 25, 2026 · 12 min read
AD PLACEHOLDER — Banner (728x90)

Season 4 brought major weapon balancing changes. We've tested every combination so you don't have to. Here are the meta builds that will win you gunfights.

AK-47 "Reaper"

S-Tier · All Ranges

The most versatile assault rifle. High damage, controllable recoil with the right attachments.

Attachments: Muzzle Brake · Long Barrel · Vertical Grip · Red Dot Sight · Extended Mag

M4A1 "Ghost"

S-Tier · Mid-to-Long Range

Laser-like accuracy. Best for players who prefer tapping and bursting over spraying.

Suppressor · Long Barrel · Angled Grip · Holo Sight · 45-Round Mag

Interceptor "Quick Scope"

A-Tier · Long Range

One-shot kill to the head. Slower fire rate but devastating in the right hands.

Sniper Scope · Bipod · Extended Barrel · Light Bolt

Vector "Rush"

A-Tier · Close Range

Insane fire rate. Melts enemies in close quarters. Requires good ammo management.

Short Barrel · No Stock · Laser Sight · 35-Round Mag · Flash Hider

SG 552 "Tap King"

B-Tier · All Ranges

Underrated. Scope + assault rifle hybrid. Great for tapping heads at long range.

Scope 2x · Foregrip · Muzzle Brake · Extended Mag
